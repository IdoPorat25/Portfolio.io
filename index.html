About Me

After several years in the dental field, I decided to pivot into data analysis—an area that has always intrigued me. Throughout my career, whether in my military service as an intelligence officer or in the healthcare sector, I’ve worked extensively with data—identifying patterns, deriving insights, and making informed decisions.

I have a strong analytical mindset, a passion for uncovering meaningful insights, and the ability to quickly adapt to new technologies. My skill set includes SQL, Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn), Power BI, and Excel, which I use to transform raw data into actionable insights.

I successfully completed the Data Analyst Expert program at John Bryce College and am eager to apply my technical expertise and problem-solving skills in a professional setting, helping businesses make data-driven decisions that drive impact.
